## A Short Field Guide to Genre Blending

Genre blending succeeds when tone and intention are clear. You can spot strong blends by asking:

- What is the emotional spine? (tension, wonder, tenderness, curiosity)
- Which genre provides structure? (mystery pacing, rom-com beat cadence, heist scaffolding)
- Which genre provides texture? (horror atmosphere, sci-fi worldbuilding, comedic relief)

When the spine, structure, and texture complement rather than compete, you get a film that feels fresh but coherent.
